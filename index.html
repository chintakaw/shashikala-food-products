Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

Shashikala Food Products began in 1986 as a small-scale business with a big dream: to bring the authentic taste of Sri Lankan spices to every kitchen. Through dedication and consistency, we have grown to capture a remarkable market share.

Our Vision: "To provide pure natural products to the nation whilst securing the high quality with the natural taste of the products at reasonable competitive prices."
